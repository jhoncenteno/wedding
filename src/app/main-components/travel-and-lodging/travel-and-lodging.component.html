How to get there

Orangerie im Schlosspark

Public transport

Do your planning for train/S-Bahn/tram/bus service here.

In Oranienburg there is a bus service. With line 824 you can get from the stop “S Oranienburg Bhf” to “Oranienburg, Breite Str.” which is in walking distance to the Orangerie. Buses in Oranienburg run until around 11 in the night and start again around 5 in the morning. To get to your hotel after 11 a taxi or Uber would be a recommendable option.

“S Oranienburg Bhf” is connected through S-Bahn line S1 (pink color) to Berlin Central Station.

Berlin Central Station is connected through the FEX airport shuttle train to BER airport.

From the airport you can also get to Oranienburg directly with regional and intercity trains (e.g. RE23 to “S Ostkreuz Bhf” and RE32 to "S Oranienburg Bhf”, IC2176 / IC2174 etc.)

RE5 goes from S+U Gesundbrunnen Bhf (a few bus stops from where we live) to Oranienburg every hour.
